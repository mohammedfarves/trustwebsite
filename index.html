About BSET

Bharathama Social Welfare and Education Trust is a registered non-profit organization committed to fostering equality, opportunity, and empowerment for all sections of society. Since our inception, we have been at the forefront of social change, working tirelessly to bridge gaps in education, healthcare, and community development.

As a CSR-focused trust, we partner with corporations, institutions, and individuals who share our vision of creating a more equitable society. Our holistic approach addresses the root causes of social issues while providing immediate relief to those in need.
